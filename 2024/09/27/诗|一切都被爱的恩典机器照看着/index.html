All Watched Over By Machines Of Loving Grace

诗 — Sep 27, 2024

高中时非常喜欢的一首小诗。当时被其中所描述的技术乐观主义深深打动了。这可是1967年的作品呀。


由爱的恩典机器照管一切(1967)

Richard Brautigan

I like to think (and the sooner the better!)

我常常想象（越快越好！）

of a cybernetic meadow

在一块电子草地上

where mammals and computers

哺乳动物和电脑

live together in mutually

共同生活在一起

programming harmony

和谐地编程

like pure water

如水般纯净

touching clear sky.

触碰清澈的天空


I like to think(right now, please!)

我常常幻想（请就在此刻实现！）

of a cybernetic forest

在一片电子森林中

filled with pines and electronics

布满了松树和电子

where deer stroll peacefully past computers

小鹿平静地在电脑边散步

as if they were flowers with spinning blossoms.

在盛开的花丛中一般


I like to think(it has to be!)

我常常期盼（它必须实现！）

of a cybernetic ecology

一个电子生态圈

where we are free of our labors

我们从自然劳作中解放出来

and joined back to nature,

回归自然

returned to our mammal

回到我们的哺乳动物

brothers and sisters,

兄弟姐妹们身边

and all watched over by machines of loving grace.

一切都被那慈爱的机器照看着


后记：有一些由这首诗名字延伸出的作品，

书《Machines of Loving Grace：The Quest for Common Ground Between Humans and Robots 》(2015)
